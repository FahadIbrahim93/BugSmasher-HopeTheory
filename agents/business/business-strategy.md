# BugSmasher Business Strategy

## Monetization Model
- **Primary**: Free-to-play with optional cosmetic upgrades
- **Secondary**: Battle Pass-style seasonal content
- **Tertiary**: Premium currency for time-savers (non-pay-to-win)

## Revenue Streams
1. **Cosmetic Skins**: Bug variants, hammer designs, particle effects ($0.99-$4.99)
2. **Season Pass**: Monthly challenges with exclusive rewards ($4.99/month)
3. **Premium Currency**: Crystals for convenience (extra lives, continues) ($1.99-$9.99)

## Market Positioning
- **Differentiator**: True skill-based progression, no pay-to-win
- **USP**: "The last bug-squashing game you'll ever need"
- **Platforms**: Web (Vercel), Mobile (iOS/Android), Steam

## User Acquisition
- **Organic**: App Store optimization, social sharing of scores
- **Community**: Discord server, Reddit engagement
- **Content**: YouTube gameplay videos, Twitch streams

## Growth Metrics
- **DAU**: Daily active users target 10k month 1
- **Retention**: Day 1: 50%, Day 7: 25%, Day 30: 10%
- **Monetization**: 2% conversion rate, $0.50 ARPDAU

## Competition Analysis
- **Candy Crush-like**: Too casual, pay-to-win
- **Geometry Dash**: Skill-based but different genre
- **Diep.io**: Similar survival but tank-focused
- **BugSmasher Position**: Unique bug-smashing + RPG progression

## Regulatory Compliance
- **COPPA**: No data collection under 13
- **GDPR**: EU data rights compliance
- **CCPA**: California privacy compliance
- **App Store**: Follow guidelines for score/achievements
