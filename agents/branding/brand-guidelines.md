# BugSmasher Brand Guidelines

## Core Identity
- **Name**: BugSmasher
- **Tagline**: "Smash Bugs, Level Up, Dominate Waves"
- **Genre**: Arcade/Action Survival Game
- **Target Audience**: Casual to mid-core gamers (18-35)

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- Primary: Neon circuit board bug with crossed hammer/sword
- Colors: Electric blue (#00F0FF) and bug green (#00FF88) on dark background
- Style: Pixel-perfect with modern glow effects

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: #00F0FF (Electric Blue), #00FF88 (Bug Green)
- **Secondary**: #FF0066 (Danger Pink), #FFD700 (Gold)
- **Background**: #0A0A1A (Deep Space Blue), #000000 (Black)
- **UI Accents**: #8A2BE2 (Electric Purple), #FF6B35 (Upgrade Orange)

### Typography
- **Headers**: 'Orbitron' or 'Share Tech Mono' (tech/sci-fi feel)
- **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Roboto' (clean readability)

### UI Principles
- CRT scanline overlay for retro feel
- Neon glow on important elements
- Pixel fonts for scores/stats
- Dark theme with vibrant accents

## Tone of Voice
- **Casual**: Friendly, encouraging, slightly competitive
- **Urgency**: "Incoming swarm!", "Wave approaching!"
- **Achievement**: "Level up!", "New record!"
- **Warning**: "Low health!", "Armored bug detected!"

## Player Archetypes
1. **Casual Crawler**: Plays 5-10 min sessions
2. **Progression Hunter**: Grinds for levels/upgrades
3. **Competitive Crusher**: Chases leaderboard scores

## Key Messaging
- "Every wave gets harder - can you survive?"
- "Upgrade your gear, smash bigger bugs"
- "From casual to hardcore - everyone's welcome"
- "No pay-to-win, just skill and strategy"
